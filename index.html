Set up StudySync

Connect to Google Sheets so your data persists across every tab and device — for free.

One-time setup (3 min)

1

Open script.google.com → click "New project"

2

Delete all code → paste the entire content of StudySync_AppsScript.js

3

Click Deploy → New deployment → Type: Web app → Execute as: Me → Who has access: Anyone

4

Click Deploy → authorize → copy the Web app URL

5

Paste the URL below and hit Connect

Your URL is saved in your browser. Data lives in Google Sheets.
